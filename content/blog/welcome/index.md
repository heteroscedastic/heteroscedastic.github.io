Welcome to the new home of **Stochastic Rambling** — statistics of life and few other outliers!

This blog has been refreshed with a modern platform built on [Hugo](https://gohugo.io) and the [HugoBlox Academic](https://hugoblox.com) theme. The new setup gives us:

- A clean, responsive design with **dark/light mode**
- Multiple **color themes** to choose from
- Built-in support for **math rendering** (LaTeX)
- **Publication management** with BibTeX support
- A proper **resume/CV** section
- **Project portfolios**

Stay tuned for posts about statistics, probability, machine learning, and the occasional outlier topic.

Happy reading!
